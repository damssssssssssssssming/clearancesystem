Data Privacy Policy


This Data Privacy Policy outlines how we, Barangay 177, collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.
We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of all personal information entrusted to us.
This policy applies to all personal information collected, used, or processed by Barangay 177, whether obtained through our website, social media channels, paper forms, or other means.
We collect personal information only for lawful purposes directly related to our functions and activities as a Barangay. Personal information may be collected through various channels, including but not limited to registration forms, surveys, and communication with individuals.

The types of personal information we may collect include, but are not limited to:
• Name
• Address
• Contact details
• Date of birth
• Identification documents
1. Providing services and assistance to residents and constituents
2. Communicating important information and updates
3. Responding to inquiries and complaints
4. Facilitating community events and programs
5. Ensuring public safety and security
6. Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.
Government agencies and authorities, as required by law;
Service providers and contractors who assist us in carrying out our functions. Other parties with the individual’s consent or as permitted by law. We implement appropriate security measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include physical, technical, and organizational safeguards.
We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Individuals have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of their personal information held by us. Requests should be submitted in writing to the Barangay office.

We may update this Data Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on our website or communicated through other appropriate channels.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Data Privacy Policy or the handling of personal information, please contact us at +639994031692.
